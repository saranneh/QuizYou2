About QuizYou

A Smarter Quiz Practice Platform

QuizYou is a web-based study and assessment platform built to help students practice course material, receive immediate feedback, and track their learning progress over time.

CS473-673 Software Engineering

Built for personalized, data-driven learning.

Students can generate timed quizzes, customize quiz settings, save results, review missed questions, and monitor performance through a dashboard. Professors can manage question banks and view class-wide analytics, while admins manage users and role-based access.

🎯

Custom Quizzes

Choose a subject, quiz length, time limit, and quiz mode to match your study goals.

⏱

Timed Practice

Practice under realistic exam conditions with timer warnings and automatic submission.

📊

Performance Dashboard

Track average score, best score, completed quizzes, mastery rank, and topic-level progress.

📝

Quiz History

Save completed quiz attempts and review your answers, correct answers, score, date, and time taken.

👩‍🏫

Professor Tools

Import questions, organize content by module and topic, control question sets, and view analytics.

🔐

Role-Based Access

Student, professor, and admin roles provide the right tools for each type of platform user.
